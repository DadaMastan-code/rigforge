RigForge — Custom PC Builder

RigForge is a free online custom PC builder. Pick every component — CPU, GPU, motherboard, RAM, storage, PSU and case — and get instant compatibility checking, wattage estimates, bottleneck analysis, and a live 3D preview of your build. No login required.

Features

  • Custom PC part picker with 60+ components across 8 categories
  • Live compatibility checking — socket, chipset, clearance, power
  • Wattage and PSU calculator
  • FPS and bottleneck performance estimates
  • Interactive 3D build preview
  • Live simulated price feed with stock tracking
  • Budget optimizer — generate a full build from a budget
  • Save and share builds with a URL
  • Multi-currency support (USD, INR, EUR, GBP and more)

How to Build a Custom PC with RigForge

Click "Start build", then choose your processor, motherboard, memory, graphics card, cooler, storage, power supply and case. RigForge checks compatibility at every step and updates the live 3D model as you pick parts. When done, share your build via a link or use the budget optimizer to auto-generate a build within your price range.
